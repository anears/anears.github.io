I am an AI researcher specializing in **medical image analysis** and **computer vision**.

At [VUNO](https://www.vuno.co/) (2021–2026), I developed deep learning models for retinal fundus screening products — **VUNO Med-Fundus AI** and its successor — working across the full range from model development and validation to release engineering. In parallel, I pursued research on the reliability of medical AI, including out-of-distribution detection, generative models for data augmentation, and label-efficient representation learning.

Our team won two international medical imaging challenges: the **Diabetic Retinopathy Analysis Challenge (DRAC) at MICCAI 2022** — ranked 1st in all three tasks, where I served as first author and led the experiments — and the **JustRAIGS glaucoma screening challenge at ISBI 2024** — ranked 1st overall, where I led the team.

I received my M.S. in Computer Software from Hanyang University (Computer Vision Lab, advised by Prof. Jongwoo Lim), where I worked on object detection, tracking, and domain generalization. Across my graduate studies and industry career, I have published 10 international papers (ICLR, BMVC, MICCAI, ISBI, Scientific Reports, and others) and hold 3 patents.
