Built an internal **LangGraph-based multi-agent harness** that automates parts of the research loop — planning experiments, running them, and drafting result reports — so that routine experiment iteration requires less researcher time.

The harness evolved through three stages: a single-script agent loop, a structured LangGraph multi-agent workflow, and finally packaging recurring procedures as reusable skills.
