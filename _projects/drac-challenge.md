Our team ranked **1st in all three tasks** (lesion segmentation, image quality assessment, DR grading) of the [Diabetic Retinopathy Analysis Challenge (DRAC)](https://drac22.grand-challenge.org/) at **MICCAI 2022**.

I was the **first author** and ran the experiments. The core theme was overcoming **data scarcity** in UW-OCTA imaging with a "bag of tricks": strong ensembling, test-time augmentation, and reliability-based pseudo-labeling {% cite kwon2022drac %}.
