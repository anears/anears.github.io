Our team ranked **1st overall** in the [JustRAIGS challenge](https://justraigs.grand-challenge.org/) (Justified Referral in AI Glaucoma Screening) at **ISBI 2024**.

I **led the team** — setting the technical direction and standardizing the collaborative experiment pipeline so that results from all members were comparable and reproducible — building on my earlier experience winning the DRAC challenge {% cite kwon2022drac %}.

Our approach was a **multi-stage, region-based pipeline**: optic disc segmentation → disc-centered cropping → an ensemble of heterogeneous backbones for fine-grained referral classification {% cite kim2024justraigs %}.
